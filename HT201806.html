Find the serial number of your Magic Keyboard, Magic Mouse, or Magic Trackpad

Learn how to locate the serial number of your Magic Keyboard, Magic Mouse, or Magic Trackpad for your Mac.

Apple wireless keyboards

Magic Keyboard

The serial number of your Magic Keyboard is on the bottom surface of the device, along with other markings. It's a string of 17 letters and numbers.
